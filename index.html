Her grandfather Mahavir Oberoi, who died when her father was very young, had produced a Bollywood film Sheba and Hercules in 1967.[5] Oberoi was educated at Arya Vidya Mandir School and Jamnabai Narsee School in Mumbai. She graduated from Mumbai University where she studied Majors in English and Psychology. While she was graduating she did a 3-month diploma course from Anupam Kher's Actor Prepares acting institute.[5] Soon after, she started working at Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment and became an Assistant Director on Kick (2014) and Housefull 3 (2016).[6] After that, she started auditioning for films and took up ads and modelling assignments before she got her debut film.[7] Career Before venturing into acting, Oberoi worked as an assistant director at the Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment productions for Kick (2014) and Housefull 3 (2016). She made her acting debut in 2019 with the romantic thriller film Yeh Saali Aashiqui, co-starring Vardhan Puri, grandson of Amrish Puri.[8] The film was directed by Cherag Ruparel and produced by Pen India Limited and Amrish Puri Films.[9] Her second film Khuda Hafiz with Vidyut Jammwal, directed by Faruk Kabir released on 14 August 2020.[10][11] Personal life It was reported that Oberoi got engaged to her long-term boyfriend actor Karam Rajpal on 15 January 2018.[12] In November 2019, she denied the reports of engagement and called these reports false.[13] Filmography Films Year Title Role Notes 2014 Kick N/A Assistant director 2016 Housefull 3 N/A 2019 Yeh Saali Aashiqui Mitali "Mitee" Deora Nominated Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut 2020 Khuda Haafiz Nargis Rajput Choudary Released on Disney+ Hotstar 2022 Khuda Haafiz: Chapter II – Agni Pariksha dagger Releasing on 17 June 2022[14] Music videos Year Title Singer(s) Label Ref. 2021 Saiyyonee Yasser Desai, Rashmeet Kaur Sony Music India [15] Soulmate Akull, Aastha Gill VYRL Originals [16] 2022 Choti Choti Galtiyan Papon DRJ Records [17] Rehna Tere Paas Armaan Malik Sony Music India [18] References "Stars who made their Bollywood debut in 2019".
